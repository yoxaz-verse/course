# Phase 5: Compensation, Growth & The Senior Operator Roadmap

**Goal:** Understand the business of being an OBAOL Operator. This phase covers how to monetize the execution pipeline, scale personal earnings, and move from a "New Entrant" to a "Senior Execution Lead."

---

## 5.1 Success-Linked Commission Structure
OBAOL Supreme does not pay for "effort" or "activity"—it pays for **Completed Execution**. This ensures that every Operator is incentivized to protect the trade from start to finish.

* **Milestone-Based Earnings:** Commission is calculated based on the successful clearing of the **6-Phase Pipeline**.
    * **Phase 04 (Audit/Verification):** Confirmation of stock and counterparty readiness.
    * **Phase 05 (Execution):** Movement of goods and subflow coordination.
    * **Phase 06 (Completion):** Final release of funds and Proof of Delivery (PoD).
* **The Commission Link:** For the specific breakdown of percentage-based earnings on bulk commodity volumes, refer directly to the official [OBAOL Commission Structure](https://www.obaol.com/commission-structure).
* **The "Execution Premium":** Operators who manage all subflows (Logistics, Packaging, and Warehousing) within the system often realize higher margins because the "Bidding System" reduces costs, leaving more room for the execution fee.

---

## 5.2 Performance Metrics & The "Trust Score"
The OBAOL dashboard tracks your performance as an Operator. High-performance data is your greatest asset for scaling.

* **Turnaround Time (TAT):** How fast do you move an inquiry from Phase 01 to Phase 04?
* **Success Ratio:** The percentage of initiated orders that reach Phase 06 (Completion).
* **Associate Feedback:** Ratings from your Transporters, Packers, and Quality Inspectors.
* **The "Trust Score":** As your score increases, the system grants you access to **Higher Volume Marketplace Leads** and **Global Import/Export Tenders** that are restricted to Senior Operators.

---

## 5.3 Scaling to Senior Operator Status
A "Serious New Entrant" focuses on one trade at a time. A **Senior Operator** manages a portfolio.

* **Portfolio Management:** Using the **Execution Panel** to oversee 5–10 bulk trades simultaneously.
* **Infrastructure Ownership:** Senior Operators often "anchor" certain regions or warehouses (the Oyo model), becoming the go-to execution point for specific commodities like Turmeric or Maize.
* **Lead Generation Chains:** By mastering the **Relationship Chain** (Phase 2), a Senior Operator ensures they always have a "warm" pipeline of requirements, moving from a "Searcher" to a "Provider."

---

## 5.4 The "Zero-Manual" Vision
The ultimate goal of the OBAOL Operator is to reach a state where the "System" does the heavy lifting:
1.  **Marketplace** finds the lead.
2.  **AI/MCP** alerts the risks.
3.  **Subflow Partners** handle the physical work.
4.  **Operator** verifies the data and triggers the completion.

## ❓ Phase 5: Q&A (Quality Assurance)

> **Q: When do I actually receive my commission?**
> **A:** Commission is typically released upon **Phase 06 (Completion)**. This occurs when the buyer has received the goods, quality is confirmed via the digital cert, and the funds are settled through the platform’s secure channels.

> **Q: Can I lose my "Operator" status?**
> **A:** Yes. If an Operator repeatedly bypasses the **Verification (Phase 04)** or attempts to take trades "offline" (untracked), they are removed to protect the safety of the OBAOL network.

> **Q: Does OBAOL provide a fixed salary?**
> **A:** No. OBAOL is a high-reward **Execution-Linked System**. You earn based on the volume you successfully move through the pipeline. This allows for unlimited earning potential based on your skill as a Digital Agro Trader.
