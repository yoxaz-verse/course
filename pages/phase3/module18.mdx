# Module 18: How to Research International Markets

**Title suggestion:**  
👉 _“How to Find Export Opportunities: Researching International Agro Markets”_

---

## 🎯 Module Objective

By the end of this video, learners will:

1. Know the main sources for international market data
2. Learn how to interpret trade data to spot trends
3. Understand import/export volumes and top trading partners
4. Practice finding top importing countries for a chosen agro product

---

## Video Structure & Script

### 1️⃣ Hook (0:00 – 0:45)

> “How do you know where to sell turmeric, cardamom, or any agro product?  
> Without research, you’re guessing — which can cost time and money.  
> In this video, I’ll show you **how to research international markets** and spot real export opportunities.”

{/* Visual */}

- Animated trader looking at multiple country flags, thinking “Where should I sell?”

---

### 2️⃣ Sources of International Market Data (0:45 – 2:00)

> “You can find trade data from official and trusted sources:
>
> - **APEDA** — Export data for Indian agro products
> - **ITC Trade Map** — Global import/export statistics
> - **FAO** — Agricultural production & trade info
> - **Customs & DGFT portals** — country-specific trade stats

> These platforms provide data on volumes, values, trends, and top trading partners.”

{/* Action */}

- Visit **one of these sites** and explore the interface

{/* Visual */}

- Screen recording or animation of website icons popping up

---

### 3️⃣ How to Interpret Trade Data (2:00 – 3:00)

> “Trade data can be overwhelming, but here’s what to focus on:
>
> - **Volume vs Value:** How much is traded and at what price
> - **Top importers:** Countries that buy the most of your product
> - **Trends:** Are imports increasing or decreasing over time?
> - **Seasonal spikes:** Certain months may have higher demand

> This information helps you choose which markets to target first.”

{/* Action */}

- Pick **one product** and note **top 3 importing countries** and their import volumes

{/* Visual */}

- Animated table showing columns: Country | Volume | Value | Trend arrow

---

### 4️⃣ Understanding Import/Export Volumes & Partners (3:00 – 3:45)

> “Top trading partners indicate **where the demand is highest**.  
> Example: India exports turmeric mainly to UAE, USA, and Germany.  
> Knowing this helps you focus research, verify buyers, and plan logistics efficiently.”

{/* Action */}

- Record **top 3 importing countries** and check if India’s supply meets their demand

{/* Visual */}

- Animated map highlighting the top importing countries with arrows from India

---

### 5️⃣ Call to Action / Task (3:45 – 4:15)

- Visit a trade data site (APEDA, ITC Trade Map, FAO, etc.)
- Identify **top 3 importing countries** for your chosen agro product
- Note **volume, value, and any observable trends**

{/* Visual */}

- Animated checklist ticking off: Visit site ✅ Pick product ✅ Note top importers ✅
