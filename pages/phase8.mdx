## 8.1 The "First 30 Minutes" (Morning Protocol)
The digital market moves faster than the physical one. Your dashboard must reflect reality by 10:00 AM.

1.  **Market Refresh:** Check the **Live Marketplace**. If the Mandi price for Maize shifted by ₹20, update your "Live Marketing" price immediately.
2.  **Subflow Audit:** Open the **Execution Panel**. Scan for "Red Alerts" (Stalled trucks, missing Quality Certs, or expired Sample requests).
3.  **Port Intelligence:** Check the **Importer Platform**. Identify which bulk commodities are landing today to predict price volatility for your active inquiries.

* **❌ Price Stalling:** Never leave a marketplace price un-updated for more than 48 hours.

## ❓ Phase 8: Q&A (Quality Assurance)

> **Q: How do I handle 5 trades at once without getting confused?**
> **A:** Each trade is a separate "Master Container" on your dashboard. Use the **Task Tracker** inside each order to see which Subflow (Packaging vs. Transport) needs your attention.

> **Q: What is the "Golden Rule" of the Digital Agro Trader?**
> **A:** **"Trust the Data, Verify the Physical."** Use the AI/MCP to find the data, but never move a ton of grain without the **Phase 04 On-Ground Audit**.

> **Q: How do I increase my earnings?**
> **A:** Master the **Inquiry Chain**. Don't just execute the "Buy"—onboard the transporter, rent the warehouse, and manage the packaging. Each added subflow increases your total execution commission.
